How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

  • The game is played on a 3x3 board grid.
  • Player 1 is X, and Player 2 is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all 9 squares are full and no player has won, the game ends in a tie.
